# About This Project and Hyperdot

## Project

**AI Visibility Checker** is an open-source tool that measures whether a business is cited and recommended by AI systems including ChatGPT (GPT-4o), Google Gemini, and Perplexity.

It was built to solve a real problem: most businesses have no idea whether AI systems know they exist — let alone recommend them. This tool makes that measurable.
